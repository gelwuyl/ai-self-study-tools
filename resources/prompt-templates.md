# Prompt Templates

Ready-to-use prompts for your data science and AI studies. Copy, paste, and fill in the `[brackets]`.

---

## 📚 Understanding Research Papers

### Get a Plain-English Summary
```
Act as a science communicator explaining AI research to a general audience.
Summarise the key idea of this paper in 3 short paragraphs: 
(1) the problem it addresses, (2) the solution proposed, (3) why it matters.
Avoid jargon. Use an everyday analogy where helpful.

Paper title: [title]
```

### Explain a Specific Section
```
I am reading [paper title] and I'm confused by [section name or concept].
Explain it in plain English as if I have no math or coding background.
Use a real-world analogy if possible.
```

### Build a Study Q&A
```
Based on the paper [title], generate 5 comprehension questions 
that test whether a beginner truly understands the core ideas.
Include the answers below each question.
```

## 🧠 Understanding Concepts

### Beginner Explanation
```
Act as an experienced data science educator teaching adult learners with no technical background.
Explain [concept] using a simple analogy from everyday life.
Keep it under 200 words and avoid technical jargon.
```

### Compare Two Concepts
```
I'm learning data science and I keep confusing [concept A] and [concept B].
Explain the difference between them using a concrete example.
Then give me a simple rule of thumb to remember which is which.
```

### Go Deeper
```
I understand the basics of [concept]. 
Now explain it one level deeper — what's actually happening "under the hood"?
Use simple language and assume I have no coding or math background.
```

## 🔍 Who Is the Expert?

### Find the Right Lens
```
Who would be the best type of expert to explain [topic] to [describe your background]?
Once you've identified the expert type, respond as that expert.
```

### Reframe for My Background
```
I come from a [your background, e.g. marketing / healthcare / education] background.
Explain [concept] using examples and analogies from my field.
```

## 📝 Study & Exam Prep

### Turn Notes into a Study Guide
```
Here are my notes from a lecture on [topic]: [paste notes]

Turn these into a structured study guide with:
- Key definitions
- The 3 most important concepts to remember
- 3 practice questions with answers
```

### Prepare for a Discussion
```
I have a class discussion coming up on [topic].
Give me 3 insightful questions I could raise and a brief point I could make for each.
Assume my audience are fellow learners who are also new to this field.
```

### Spot My Gaps
```
Here is my understanding of [concept]: [write your explanation in your own words]

What have I got right? What am I missing or misunderstanding?
Be specific and kind — I'm still learning.
```

## 🤖 Meta Prompts (Ask AI to Help You Prompt)

### Generate a Better Prompt
```
I want to ask an AI about [your topic].
Help me write a clear, detailed prompt that will get the best possible answer.
My goal is: [what you're trying to achieve]
My background: [your level and context]
```

### Improve a Prompt You Already Have
```
Here is a prompt I wrote: [your prompt]

How can I improve it to get a clearer, more useful answer?
Rewrite it for me using best practices for AI prompting.
```

---

## 💬 General Tips

- **Be specific** — the more detail you give, the better the response
- **Set the persona** — "Act as a [role]" dramatically improves output quality
- **State your level** — always mention that you're a beginner or your background
- **Ask for analogies** — "Use an everyday analogy" almost always helps
- **Iterate** — if the first answer isn't quite right, say "that's a bit too technical, can you simplify?"
